What it does

Everything Claude.ai is missing.

📁

Nested folders

Organise your Claude conversations into folders and subfolders. Drag to reorder. Assign any chat in one click — right from the claude.ai sidebar.

⚡

Prompt library

Save reusable prompts with {{variable}} slots. Press Ctrl+Shift+Space anywhere on claude.ai to search and insert instantly.

↓

Markdown export

Download any conversation as a clean .md file. Works perfectly with Obsidian, Notion, and any text editor. One click, no copy-paste.

🔒

Your data never leaves your browser.

Tidewell for Claude is fully local-first. There are no servers, no accounts, and no syncing. Everything is stored in your browser's own storage.

  • No account required
  • No cloud storage
  • No telemetry or analytics
  • Open to inspection
  • Works offline
